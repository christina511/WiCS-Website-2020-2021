Women in Computer Science

Stony Brook University

We are an organization whose mission is to provide encouragement and support to women, both graduate and undergraduate, in the field of computing. We aim to foster understanding between men and women in Computer Science and to improve women's involvement in the field. We hold events, both academic and social in nature, and work with other groups and the Computer Science department.

We are an official ACM-W chapter.

August WiCSter of the Month

Carolyn Bremer

Please meet our August WiCSter of the month, Carolyn Bremer!


Carolyn is a senior majoring in Computer Science and is passionate about inspiring women in STEM. She’s an active member of WiCS and was previously the Webmaster. Currently, she’s the VP of the Society of Women Engineers, an Honors College member, and part of the Vertically Integrated Project team Web Genomics. This summer she’s interning at Discover Financial Services as a Software Engineer. When she’s not studying, Carolyn loves photography, going to concerts, and hanging out with friends.

Favorite Language: Python
